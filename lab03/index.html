Origin Story

Origin Story

Taylor grew up in Pennsylvania, but she emerged so quickly in her childhood as a star that her parents sold their house when she was 13 to move to Hendersonville, Tennessee where she could focus more on her career. At 14, she had already signed with Sony as a songwriter!
